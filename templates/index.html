About This Page:

The data used in creation of this page originated on Kaggle.com. and included thorough details on accidents reported at different types of amusement businesses in the United States from the year 1986 to 2009. Above you can find an interactive map which allows you to search through the different types of businesses. Click on the dots on the map to view the location, date and details on the accidents reported. These details include ride type, number injured, and injury description. The map filtering tool also filters the corresponding scatter plot which shows the number of injuries on different rides at your selected business type. There is a scatter plot which summarizes the incident occurrences at each business type from the years 1986 to 2009. The bar graph summarizes the total number of injuries reported at each business type from the years 1986 to 2009.
